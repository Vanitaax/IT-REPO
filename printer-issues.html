Printer Not Working?

If you're having trouble printing, please follow these troubleshooting steps:

  • Ensure the printer is powered on and connected to the network.
  • Check for any paper jams or low ink/toner alerts.
  • Restart both your computer and the printer.
  • Verify the correct printer is selected on your device.
  • Run the Windows printer troubleshooter.
