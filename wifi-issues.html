Having Trouble Connecting to Wi-Fi?

Follow these steps to try and fix your Wi-Fi connectivity issues:

  • Make sure your Wi-Fi is turned on and you're connected to the correct network.
  • Restart your device and router (if accessible).
  • Forget the network and reconnect with the correct password.
  • Ensure Airplane mode is turned off.
  • Try connecting to a different Wi-Fi network to isolate the issue.
